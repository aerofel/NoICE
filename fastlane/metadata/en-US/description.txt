No-ICE is the streamlined hold-over time companion built for professional pilots. Get instant access to official FAA and Transport Canada de-icing tables, track your hold-over times with precision, and stay informed from cockpit to lock screen.

Whether you're operating in light freezing drizzle or heavy snow, No-ICE filters through complex hold-over time tables in seconds — so you can focus on what matters: a safe departure.

OFFICIAL DATA, ALWAYS CURRENT
- FAA and Transport Canada (TCA) hold-over time tables
- Type I, II, III, and IV fluid coverage
- All weather conditions: snow, freezing rain, freezing drizzle, frost, and more
- Multiple fluid concentrations and temperature ranges

PRECISION TIMER
- One-tap start, pause, and resume with animated visual feedback
- Elapsed time and UTC/Zulu start time displayed at a glance
- Assured time (green) and Limit time (orange) calculated in real time
- Automatic flaps/slats factor (0.76 reduction) when extended
- Freeze timestamps to log each check during multi-step procedures

LIVE ACTIVITY & DYNAMIC ISLAND
- Monitor your hold-over countdown directly from the lock screen
- Dynamic Island integration for at-a-glance status
- Visual progress bar with color-coded assured and limit zones
- Bar turns fully red when hold-over time is exceeded
- All key data visible without opening the app

BUILT FOR THE FLIGHT DECK
- Celsius and Fahrenheit support
- Smart weather filtering by precipitation type and intensity
- Persistent state — resume exactly where you left off after a restart
- Haptic feedback for confident, eyes-free operation
- Optimized for iPhone and iPad
